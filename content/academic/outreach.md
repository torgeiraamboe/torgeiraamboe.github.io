### Researchers' night

Researchers' night is an evening of science outreach at NTNU, where over 1000 high school students come to learn and interact with labs, hear talks, and talk with researchers on curated stands. More information at [their webpage](https://www.ntnu.no/forskningsdagene/night). 

- 2024: *Quantum and topological games*. Three challenging non-strandard versions of tic-tac-toe. 
- 2023: *Why are soap bubbles round?* On minimal surfaces and energy optimization.
- 2022: *The worlds most difficult equation*. A visual introduction to the Navier--Stokes equation. 
- 2022: [Forskningstorget](https://www.ntnu.no/forskningsdagene/torget)
