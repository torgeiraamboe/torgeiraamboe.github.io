My name is Torgeir Aambø and I am a researcher in Operations Research at the Norwegian Defence Research Establishment (FFI). 

I did my PhD in pure mathematics at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), where I was part of the project ["Tensor triangulated geometry in Trondheim"](https://folk.ntnu.no/drewkh/tt.html) led by professor [Drew Heard](https://folk.ntnu.no/drewkh/). My research focused on chromatic and stable homotopy theory and its interactions with homological algebra.  

In my spare time I play drums in some [bands]({{<ref "misc/../../misc/music_and_other.md" >}}), and I like [reading]({{<ref "have-read">}}), [bouldering, hiking and running](https://www.strava.com/athletes/104823864).
