My name is Torgeir Aambø. I’m currently a PhD student at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) in Trondheim, where I am part of the project ["Tensor triangulated geometry in Trondheim"](https://folk.ntnu.no/drewkh/tt.html) supervised by [Drew Heard](https://folk.ntnu.no/drewkh/). 

This site is meant to host my journey through the three pillars of academia: reading, writing and speaking. Here you will find my projects, talks I have held and been to, various writings, things I read as well as my blog. On the blog you will find posts about what I have been learning (mostly mathematics). 

I also organize the [Young Homotopy-theorist Online Meeting](https://younghomseminar.github.io/) together with [Julie Rasmusen](https://sites.google.com/view/julierasmusen) and the [PITA seminar](https://wiki.math.ntnu.no/seminar/pita) together with [Endre Rundsveen](https://www.ntnu.no/ansatte/endre.s.rundsveen). I also maintain a [thesis archive](https://fagkom.github.io/) (mostly bachelor and master) written by students of mathematics and physics at NTNU – in particular people who have been a part of Delta, the student association for mathematics and physics.
